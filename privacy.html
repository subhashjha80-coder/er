PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy explains how ExamReady collects, stores, and handles information when you use our platform. We believe in full transparency about what data stays on your device and how nothing is shared with third parties.

Last Updated: April 2026
Effective Immediately
CBSE Students · Class 9–12
Section 1

Overview & Scope

ExamReady is a free educational platform designed for CBSE students in Class 9 to 12. This Privacy Policy applies to all users who visit our website, use our quiz tools, access PDF resources, read solution articles, or interact with any feature of the platform.

By using ExamReady, you agree to the data practices described in this document. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the site. We update this policy periodically and will always post the most current version on this page.

This policy covers: what information we store, how that storage works, what third-party services may be involved, and what rights you have as a user.

Section 2

Information We Store

ExamReady does not require user registration, login, or any account creation. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment details.

The following data may be stored locally in your browser using localStorage — this means it stays on your own device and is never sent to a server or third party:

  • Quiz content created or edited by the site administrator
  • PDF resource entries and their associated URLs or Google Drive links
  • Solution articles published via the admin panel
  • Site configuration settings such as branding, colors, and navigation
  • Admin session authentication (stored in sessionStorage, cleared when you close the tab)
  • Announcement bar content, feature toggles, and logo images (if uploaded)

Important: All of this data resides entirely in your own browser. If you clear your browser's local storage or cache, this content will be removed from that browser.

Section 3

How Browser Storage Works

ExamReady uses two native browser storage mechanisms: localStorage for persistent content (quizzes, PDFs, settings) and sessionStorage for temporary admin authentication that clears when the browser tab is closed.

Data stored this way never leaves your device automatically, is not transmitted to any ExamReady server, is not accessible by other websites due to browser same-origin policies, can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data, and may be shared across tabs within the same browser on the same device.

If an administrator uses the Export Data feature, a JSON file is generated and downloaded to their device. This file contains all stored content and should be handled responsibly.

Section 4

Third-Party Services & External Links

Some features of ExamReady connect to external platforms. We have no control over these third-party services and are not responsible for their data practices. Third-party services that may be involved include:

  • Google Drive: PDF files may be hosted on Google Drive and embedded via iframe. When you open a PDF preview, Google's servers serve that file and Google's privacy policy applies.
  • Google Fonts: We use Google Fonts to display typography. Google may log font requests from your browser.
  • YouTube: Our YouTube channel (@EXAMREADY_STUDY) is hosted by Google/YouTube. Clicking YouTube links is governed by YouTube's privacy policy.
  • Instagram: Our Instagram page (@examready.co) is hosted by Meta. Visiting it is governed by Meta's privacy policy.

We do not embed advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics scripts on ExamReady pages.

Section 5

Student Use & Data Safety

ExamReady is designed for students aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are under 13, please use this platform with parental or guardian supervision.

Students should avoid entering real personal information in quiz titles, notes, or file names. They should not upload sensitive personal documents through the file upload feature, and should use the platform on a personal or trusted device rather than shared public computers.

No user behavior, quiz scores, or study habits are tracked or stored anywhere other than your own local device. ExamReady is a study tool, not a data-collection platform.

Section 6

Administrator Data Responsibilities

If you use the ExamReady admin panel to manage content, you accept responsibility for the material you add to the platform. Administrators should ensure:

  • All uploaded PDF links and file URLs are from sources you have permission to share
  • Quiz questions and solution content do not infringe on third-party copyright
  • The admin password is kept secure and not shared with unauthorized individuals
  • Exported data backups (JSON files) are stored securely on your own device
  • Student-facing content is accurate, appropriate, and educationally responsible

Since all data is browser-local, the admin is the custodian of that data on their device. ExamReady cannot access, retrieve, or delete your stored content remotely.

Section 7

Cookies & Tracking

ExamReady does not use first-party cookies for tracking, advertising, or user identification. We do not place any analytics tracking scripts on our pages.

Browser storage (localStorage and sessionStorage) is used strictly for site functionality, not tracking. These are not cookies in the traditional sense — they cannot be sent with HTTP requests and are inaccessible to external servers.

Third-party services accessed via links (Google Drive, YouTube, Instagram) may set their own cookies in your browser. These are outside our control and governed by those platforms' policies.

Section 8

Your Rights & Data Control

Because ExamReady stores all data locally on your device, you have complete control at all times:

  • Access: View all stored data using your browser's developer tools under Application > Local Storage
  • Delete: Clear your browser's site data or local storage to remove all ExamReady content instantly
  • Export: Admins can export all content as a JSON backup file using Settings > Export
  • Reset: The admin panel includes a "Reset to Defaults" option that wipes all custom content
  • Portability: Exported JSON files can be imported on another device to transfer content

Since we do not operate a user database or collect personal information, there is no need to submit a formal data deletion request to us.

Section 9

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage users and administrators to review this policy periodically. Continued use of ExamReady after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

If you have questions about this policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through our social media channels listed on this page.
