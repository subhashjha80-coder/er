This disclaimer outlines the limitations of ExamReady's responsibilities regarding the accuracy of content, external links, quiz results, and academic guidance provided through the platform. Please read carefully before relying on any content for official academic purposes.

Section 1

No Official Affiliation

ExamReady is an independent, student-focused educational platform. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT), any state board, or any school or coaching institution unless explicitly stated on the platform.

References to CBSE, NCERT, or board examinations on this platform are for descriptive educational context only. Any material labeled as a CBSE or NCERT resource is shared for revision support and does not represent official publication or certification by those bodies.

Always refer to official CBSE (cbse.gov.in) and NCERT (ncert.nic.in) websites for authoritative syllabuses, date sheets, sample papers, and academic updates.

Section 2

Accuracy of Materials

While ExamReady makes reasonable efforts to ensure that quiz questions, solution articles, and study materials are accurate and educationally relevant, we do not guarantee the correctness, completeness, or currency of any content on the platform.

The following types of content should be independently verified by users:

  • Quiz answers and explanations — cross-check with your textbook or teacher
  • Solution articles — treat as revision support, not a definitive answer key
  • Syllabus references — confirm with the current official CBSE syllabus document
  • Exam patterns and marking schemes — verify with the official CBSE sample paper
  • Any numerical answers or formula-based solutions in science and mathematics

Content on this platform may not reflect the most current syllabus revisions or CBSE notification changes. It is the user's responsibility to check for updates through official channels.

Section 3

External File Hosting

Many PDFs and study materials on ExamReady are hosted on third-party platforms, primarily Google Drive. ExamReady acts only as a linking and organizational interface — it does not host, control, or modify the content of these files directly.

Regarding externally hosted files:

  • File availability depends entirely on the hosting provider (e.g., Google Drive). If a file is deleted or the link expires, ExamReady cannot restore it
  • Preview speed, download availability, and embed functionality depend on the hosting platform and your internet connection
  • ExamReady is not responsible for any virus, malware, or inappropriate content found in files hosted by third-party providers
  • Files hosted externally are governed by the terms and policies of their respective hosting providers

If you encounter a broken link or a file that appears inappropriate, please contact us through our social media channels so we can investigate and remove it if necessary.

Section 4

Use at Your Own Judgment

ExamReady is best described as a study companion, not an official exam preparation authority. Students and educators use the platform at their own discretion and judgment.

We strongly recommend that students:

  • Use ExamReady alongside — not as a replacement for — their NCERT textbooks and classroom notes
  • Discuss any unclear solutions or quiz explanations with their subject teachers
  • Follow their school's study plan and prioritize official CBSE-provided sample papers for board examination practice
  • Not base critical academic decisions (such as which chapters to skip) solely on resource availability or quiz scores on this platform

Quiz scores, performance metrics, and resource lists on ExamReady are informal indicators only. They should never be interpreted as official assessments or predictions of board examination performance.

Section 5

No Professional or Institutional Advice

ExamReady does not provide career counseling, stream selection advice, institutional admission guidance, or legal academic advice. Any suggestions embedded in quiz feedback, solution articles, or study tips are informal and educational in nature only.

For decisions involving stream selection, career paths, university applications, or board examination accommodations, please consult your school counselor, a qualified education advisor, or official CBSE documentation.

Section 6

Quiz Content Disclaimer

Quizzes on ExamReady are created and published by administrators for revision and self-assessment purposes. The questions may be original or adapted from publicly available educational resources. They are not official CBSE question papers.

Quiz results are calculated in real time in your browser and are not stored on any server. They are not permanent records and cannot be used as official academic credentials. Each quiz session is independent and results are not shared with schools, boards, or any third party.

If you notice a factual error in any quiz question, please reach out to us through our Instagram or YouTube channel so we can correct it promptly.
